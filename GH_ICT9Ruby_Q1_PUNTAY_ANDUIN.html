Your name is a Japanese animated movie made by Makoto Shinkai. His other works include Weathering in You, The Garden of Words, and Suzume.

Why Watch Your Name?

Your Name is a Japanese animated romance movie about a boy from 2016 and a girl from 2013. The main plot is that they share a magical connection when they start swapping bodies, Taki and Mitsuha have to live eachother's lives. But things get complicated once they try to meet eachother in person.
